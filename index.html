What is CHA◎?

CHA◎ is a 6-10 player social deduction and strategy game set in the mythological world of the Investiture of the Gods. Players take on roles of gods, immortals, demons, and mortals from ancient Chinese mythology, each with unique abilities tied to I Ching hexagrams.

The game unfolds through cycles of Night Actions and Day Discussions/Voting, where three factions vie for control:

H Heaven (Order)

The Order of Heaven seeks to maintain cosmic balance by eliminating the Chaos faction.

C Chasm (Chaos)

The Rebellion of Chaos fights against heavenly mandates, pursuing absolute freedom.

A Anarchy (Mortals)

Mortals navigate the conflict with personal agendas, choosing sides for their own survival.

The Fourfold Philosophy

C

CHASM

The Rebellion of Chaos

Representing the截教 (Jie Jiao) faction, born from the裂隙 (rift) in heavenly order. They embrace chaos as a path to true freedom, willing to descend into the abyss to break predetermined fate.

H

HEAVEN

The Mandate of Order

Representing the闡教 (Chan Jiao) faction, upholders of天命 (heavenly mandate). They are the embodiment of cosmic rules, celestial light pursuing stability and ideal harmony.

A

ANARCHY

The Will of Mortals

Representing凡人 (mortals) caught between godly conflicts. Each mortal is driven by personal ambition, revenge, or sacrifice, seeking their own path in the chaotic world.

◎

TAIJITU

The Eternal Cycle

The game's core symbol representing beginning, ending, and endless循环 (cycle). Each conclusion becomes the next beginning in an eternal dance of yin and yang.

Gameplay Flow

1

The Beginning

Players receive secret roles with unique abilities and I Ching hexagrams.申公豹 (Shen Gongbao) may disrupt a player's first night.

2

Night of Shadows

Gods and demons awaken in sequence: 通天教主 → 楊戩 → 姜子牙 → 鄧嬋玉 → 聞仲 → 妲己 → 雷震子.

3

Day of Judgment

Reveal night results, debate identities, and vote to exile suspects. 紂王 (King Zhou) has 2 votes. 伯邑考 (Bo Yikao) can sacrifice to take another with him.

4

Ascension Scroll

Eliminated souls enter the封神榜 (Ascension Scroll), sending one-word clues to influence the living in subsequent nights.

◎

Eternal Cycle

When a faction achieves victory, the cycle resets. The fallen become legends, and new stories begin with different roles and alliances.

Core Rules

Player Count

6-10 players (optimized for 8-10)

Game Duration

45-60 minutes per session

Victory Conditions

  • Heaven: Eliminate all Chasm members
  • Chasm: Eliminate all Heaven members
  • Mortals: Achieve personal objectives

Night Order

  1. 通天教主 (Lord Tongtian)
  2. 楊戩 (Yang Jian)
  3. 姜子牙 (Jiang Ziya)
  4. 鄧嬋玉 (Deng Chanyu)
  5. 聞仲 (Wen Zhong)
  6. 妲己 (Daji)
  7. 雷震子 (Lei Zhenzi)

Key Mechanics

Opening Disruption

申公豹 (Shen Gongbao) can disrupt a player's first night ability, adding an element of uncertainty from the very start.

Guard Protection

楊戩's Guard can block one attack (except unblockable attacks like 通天教主's Sword Array).

Ascension Scroll

Dead players send one-word clues to influence the living, maintaining engagement.

I Ching Integration

Each character's hexagram provides thematic depth and can be used for divination.
